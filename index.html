Click groups of colors to explode them. Try to form large groups for score multipliers. Avoid leaving any stragglers and try to use as few clicks as possible. The first couple of levels are pretty easy, then the blocks get smaller and more colors are added. There are 10 levels and 1 bonus level. Most people can't get past level 6.
